STORY

We have run S.E.P. Project for 3 consecutive years, starting from 2012.

S.E.P. 2015 Project aims to enhance practical sexuality knowledge, raise reality awareness and social responsibility, as well as global mindset for Vietnamese youth, particularly HCMC students.

Our key activities include informative seminars and practical workshops exclusive for HCMC high school students, field trips to HIV villages and shelters, and cultural events with participation of international volunteers from AIESEC International network.

BUSINESS INFO

⚑
Started on March 1, 2012
⛯
S.E.P. provides:
- informative seminars and practical workshops on sex education for high school students.
- field trips to shelters/ HIV villages, for beneficiaries to receive financial support and societal connection, for participants to have reality awareness and social responsibilities.
- sharings and interaction between local youth and international volunteers, via culture events, to exchange knowledge and perspectives on sex, open mindset by understanding other countries' culture and reality on sex issues.
- a 3-day Camp as a platform for youth to raise voice on sex issues, raise people's attention, and showcase its impact to the society.
